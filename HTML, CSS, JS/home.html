WE ARE PALORANT!

Blend your style and skill on the global
competitive stage. 13 rounds to attack
and defend, master fierce gunplay and
tactical abilities to dominate the battlefield.
Every round is a test of wits and precision,
with only one life, outsmart opponents
to survive. Take down your enemies in
Competitive, Unranked, Deathmatch, or
Spike Rush modes, and prove you have
what it takes to rise above the rest.

NEON

Neon is a lightning-fast duelist who
redefines speed and aggression on the
battlefield. As one of PALORANT’s BEST
AGENTS, she dominates with raw energy,
sprinting past enemies and striking before
they even realize she’s there. Her
electrifying abilities allow for rapid
rotations, chaotic flanks, and high-impact
entries that leave no room for hesitation.
Whether she’s zipping through Fast Lane or
unleashing her dead Overdrive, Neon turns
every round into a high-voltage showcase
of precision and power.
