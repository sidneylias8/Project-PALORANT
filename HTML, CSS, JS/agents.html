NEON

DUELIST

Neon is a fast and electrifying agent from the Philippines. She uses her speed to rush enemies, slide past danger, and strike with powerful electric attacks. Her agility makes her hard to hit, and she can quickly turn the tide of battle with her burst movements. Using energy walls and stuns, she controls space while keeping pressure on the enemy. Neon is perfect for players who love aggressive, fast-paced gameplay and want to stay one step ahead of their opponents.

JETTLEE

DUELIST

Jettlee is a swift and electrifying agent hailing from South Korea. Harnessing the power of wind, she dashes through the battlefield, evading danger and striking enemies with unparalleled precision. Her agility allows her to control the flow of combat, making her a formidable opponent who can swiftly turn the tide of any encounter. Jettlee is perfect for players who thrive on aggressive, high-speed gameplay and seek to outmaneuver their adversaries at every turn.

BRIMSMOKE

CONTROLLER

Brimsmoke is a tactical and relentless agent representing the raw power of discipline and fire. With his arsenal of precision airstrikes and smokescreens, he dominates the battlefield by controlling vision and space. His grounded nature allows teams to execute plans efficiently, while his commanding presence ensures every fight is on his terms. Brimsmoke is ideal for players who favor calculated plays, strong area control, and leading their squad to victory through fire and force.

SHYPIRE

SENTINEL

Shypire is a master of surveillance and secrets, operating silently from the shadows with sharp intellect. Hailing from Morocco, he gathers intel and locks down areas using advanced high-tech gadgets. He sees what others miss, tracking enemies and setting deadly traps for careless steps. His calm, calculated style perfectly suits players who control the battlefield through information, patience, and psychological warfare.

KOYA

INITIATOR

Koya is a futuristic enforcer built to neutralize threats and suppress chaos. Designed for frontline combat, he disables abilities and clears the way with brutal efficiency. Koya thrives in high-pressure situations, using disruptive tech and instincts to control any fight. Whether leading a push or shutting down a flank, Koya is ideal for players who love to charge in, break momentum, and be the unstoppable force their team relies on.
